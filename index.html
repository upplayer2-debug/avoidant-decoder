About us

Built by a team of psychologists, not by another relationship influencer.

This system was created from repeated patterns we kept seeing in real anxious–avoidant relationships: the silence after closeness, the hot-cold cycle, the return that reopens hope, and the moment people no longer know whether to stay or leave.

10+years of attachment-focused practice
500+one-on-one sessions reviewed across avoidant dynamics
25+real-life situations decoded inside the system

We are a group of psychologists and relationship researchers who study attachment patterns, emotional regulation, and the way inconsistent closeness changes a person’s nervous system.

After hundreds of 1:1 sessions, we noticed the same gap: people usually understand the theory, but they still do not know what to do when their avoidant partner goes quiet, returns warm, avoids commitment, or keeps repeating the same cycle.

The Avoidant Decision System was built to close that gap: not to diagnose your partner, not to force you to stay, and not to give generic “give space” advice — but to help you see the behavior clearly and respond from data instead of panic.
